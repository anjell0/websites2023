Kitty Goes To The Vet

Once upon a time, there was a cat so sweet,
Whose fur was soft and fluffy, and always looked neat.
But one day she felt sick, and her meows were low,
So her owner took her to the doctor, and away she’d go.

The doctor was kind, with a smile on his face,
He listened to the kitty's heart, with a gentle embrace.
He looked in her eyes, and he listened to her purr,
And he said, "My dear cat, you don't need a cure."

The kitty was relieved, and she let out a purr,
For she was so happy, her heart began to blur.
The doctor just smiled, and he gave her a treat,
And he said, "My dear cat, you're as healthy as can be."

So the kitty went home, with a skip in her step,
Her tail held high, and her heart full of pep.
And she lived happily, with her owner by her side,
And she’d never forget the day she went to the doctor, with pride.
