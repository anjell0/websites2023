Kitty Goes To School

Once upon a time, a kitty with fur so fine,
Decided to go to school, and learn new things this time.
She packed her small backpack, with a notebook and a pen,
And headed to her first day, with a smile and a wag of her tail then.

She walked into the classroom, with eyes so bright and bold,
And greeted all her classmates, young and old.
The teacher was friendly, and taught her how to spell,
And the kitty felt so happy, she could hardly tell.

She learned about numbers, and how to write her name,
And she made many new friends, with the same goal to aim.
She played at recess, and climbed on the jungle gym,
And had so much fun, she could hardly keep it in.

So at the end of the day, the kitty with a grin,
Went home to her owner, and couldn't wait to begin.
She told her about school, and all the fun she had,
And she was so excited, for tomorrow she would be back!
