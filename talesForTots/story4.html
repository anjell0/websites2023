Kitty Loses A Tooth

Once upon a time, in a house so grand,
Lived a kitty with a tooth that wouldn't stand.
She ate her treats and chews, with a meow and a grin,
But one day, she noticed, her tooth had come loose within.

She wiggled and jiggled, and tried to make it stay,
But it wouldn't listen, and continued to sway.
She meowed to her owners, with a look of such fright,
And they knew just what to do, to make everything alright.

They took her to the vet, with a calm and gentle touch,
And the kitty was brave, and didn't complain too much.
The vet pulled her tooth, with a quick and gentle hand,
And the kitty was relieved, and back to normal in no time, oh so grand.

She went home with a treat, and a wag of her tail,
And she knew that she was loved, and would never fail.
She slept soundly that night, with a purr so warm and bright,
And she smiled in her dreams, knowing everything was just right.
