Kitty's Big Debut

Once upon a time, in a home so bright,
Lived a kitty with a talent that shone so light.
She could sing a tune, with a meow so clear,
But when it came to crowds, she'd freeze with fear.

She'd hide under the bed, and shake with fright,
At the thought of performing, in the spotlight that night.
But her owners encouraged her, with love and with cheer,
And they told her that she had nothing to fear.

So she practiced and practiced, until she was bold,
And she took to the stage, with a confidence so bold.
She meowed her song, with a voice so pure,
And the crowd went wild, with a cheer that was so sure.

She basked in the applause, and preened with delight,
And she knew that she had conquered, her stage fright that night.
From that moment on, she sang with such grace,
And she knew that she was loved, in this special place.
