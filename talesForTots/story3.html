A New Kitty For Kitty?!

Once upon a time, in a home so bright,
Lived a kitty with fur so soft and light.
She was the queen of the house, with a purr so sweet,
And she always got her way, with a gentle meow and a treat.

One day, her owners came home with a surprise,
A tiny, little baby, with eyes so blue and bright.
The kitty was curious, and a little bit scared,
But she knew that this new arrival, was someone to be cared.

She sniffed at the baby, and gave him a lick,
And she knew that from that moment, their friendship would click.
She watched him as he slept, with a contented purr,
And she knew that she would always, be his protector and friend for sure.

So the kitty and the baby, grew up day by day,
Playing and napping, in the most loving way.
And though the kitty was still the queen of the house,
She made room in her heart, for her baby brother, and that was no mouse.
