About

Tales for Tots is a platform for short, children's stories that are designed to inspire and encourage kids to overcome challenges and find solutions to everyday problems. The stories feature relatable characters and situations that children can easily identify with, making it easier for them to understand and apply the lessons learned in their own lives. Whether it's facing fear, building confidence, or simply learning the importance of kindness and empathy, the website offers a fun and engaging way for kids to learn and grow. With its eye-catching titles and imaginative tales, kids are sure to be entertained and inspired.

It is JourneyBoiz’s mission to create inspirational, wondrous worlds and stories that under represented groups can relate to. It is hard growing up and not seeing yourself on your TV screen, metaphorically. Some of the brightest and most imaginative minds are left unattended, causing a stunt in the world’s growth in imagination and creativity. JourneyBoiz wishes to inspire each and every underrepresented group to be the best they can be and overcome their life challenges no matter how high the hurdle.

JourneyBoiz hopes that through video games, animation, stories, and even music, minds less pandered to will develop a sense of creativity and inspiration to allow them to succeed in whatever endeavors and aspirations they may hold in their heart. Not only is the accomplishment important, but remaining true to their morals and values will hopefully give them a sense of fulfillment. JourneyBoiz wishes every mind from every background, skin color, orientation, and gender to be as great as they aspire to be.
